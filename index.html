Sidewinder is a timeseries database designed for speed and scalability. It's purpose is to provide storage, retrieval and analysis for time series data in near real-time. It can be used to power dashboards and real-time correlations for time series data at scale. Sidewinder was designed for IoT (Internet of Things), Application Metrics and Analytics.
